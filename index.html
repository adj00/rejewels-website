Who We Are

A vertically integrated house of fine jewellery.

Ré Jewels is a jewellery manufacturing company founded in 2005 by Mr. Rajeev Jain. We work with our in-house karigars to produce the finest jewellery. We strive to deliver quality products to retailers all over India and abroad.

Our strengths lie in our in-house manufacturing and on-time deliveries — keeping in mind the high standards of finishing required for every piece.
