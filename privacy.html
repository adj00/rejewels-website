Ré Jewels (RE Jewels Pvt. Ltd.) respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information we collect

This site does not sell online and does not require an account. The only personal information we collect is what you choose to send us through the enquiry form, namely:

  • Your name
  • Your company or store name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • The message you write to us

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your wholesale enquiry and to correspond with you about it. We do not sell your information to third parties.

How it is processed

Enquiry submissions are delivered to us by email through a third-party form service (Formspree). Your details are transmitted to that service in order to reach our team's inbox. We also retain the resulting emails so we can follow up with you.

Cookies & analytics

This website does not set its own tracking cookies. If we add analytics or embedded maps in future, this policy will be updated to reflect it.
