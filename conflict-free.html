Ré Jewels is committed to sourcing diamonds and gemstones responsibly, and to supplying only stones that are, to the best of our knowledge, free of conflict.

Our commitment

We intend to work with suppliers who share our commitment to ethical sourcing, and to align our practices with recognised industry standards for the diamond trade, including the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme and the associated System of Warranties.

Certification

Diamonds in our jewellery can be independently graded by the International Gemological Institute (IGI), giving our buyers documentation they can trust at every price point.

Supplier warranties

Where applicable, we seek warranties from our suppliers confirming that the stones they provide are sourced from legitimate, conflict-free channels.

Questions

For details of our sourcing and certification for a specific order, please contact us at info@rejewels.com.
